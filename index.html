About Ben

Ben Martin worked as a barista in the heart of Virginia for two years. His experience in the coffee business, along with nearly a decade in the food and service industry fueled a passion for hospitality and quality. As a dedicated coffee brewer, Ben has narrowed his process down to a science, and seeks to make every brew his next best cup. As an Army reservist, Ben deployed to the Middle East in 2022 and made a reputation for himself as the base barista. For Christmas and Thanksgiving overseas, he set up a make-shift coffee bar and provided free, high-quality pourovers for dozens of international service members as they celebrated the holidays.


- "The most important thing is that great coffee can go anywhere."
